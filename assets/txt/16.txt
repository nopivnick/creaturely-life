She does not mean this, both in the sense that who she is cannot be understood by whom she has become now during these days at his side, and also in the sense that she would not, were the world within her grasp to fashion, mean these events to compass their lives. A third sense buried in this simple phrase she can recognize but not articulate, instead what comes to mind is a bug within a milkweed husk, something she saw once surely, perhaps as a girl, the strange feeling of the thing between moist and dry, vaguely sticky, the scent of honey, mold and spice not unlike that of the first mummy she ever saw, the bandaged creature free of its carapace in the dim basement of a museum, that one an “elite woman” according to the caption, the field trip just a week after her first period, she still unsure then what had happened.
